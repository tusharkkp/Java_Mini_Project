# 📋 Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. Regardless of background, experience level, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or any other protected characteristic, everyone is welcome in this community.

We are dedicated to:
- Creating a respectful and professional environment
- Welcoming diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing
- Maintaining open communication
- Resolving conflicts constructively

---

## 🤝 Expected Behavior

All members of our community are expected to:

### Be Respectful
- ✅ Treat all people with respect and dignity
- ✅ Listen actively to others' perspectives
- ✅ Acknowledge different viewpoints and experiences
- ✅ Use inclusive language
- ✅ Be patient and kind

### Be Professional
- ✅ Keep discussions focused on the project
- ✅ Use constructive feedback when providing criticism
- ✅ Avoid making assumptions about people
- ✅ Take responsibility for your words and actions
- ✅ Maintain professional communication in all channels

### Be Collaborative
- ✅ Help others learn and grow
- ✅ Share knowledge generously
- ✅ Give credit where it's due
- ✅ Work together toward common goals
- ✅ Report issues through proper channels

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are absolutely unacceptable:

### Harassment
- ❌ Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- ❌ Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- ❌ Deliberate intimidation or threats
- ❌ Stalking or doxxing (publishing private information)
- ❌ Repeated unsolicited messages

### Discrimination
- ❌ Exclusionary language or behavior
- ❌ Prejudice based on any protected characteristic
- ❌ Denying opportunities based on identity
- ❌ Gatekeeping or power-based exclusion

### Abuse
- ❌ Personal attacks or insults
- ❌ Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- ❌ Spam or off-topic flooding
- ❌ Spreading misinformation
- ❌ Violence or violent threats

### Violations of Privacy
- ❌ Sharing others' private information without consent
- ❌ Recording conversations without consent
- ❌ Publishing private communications

---

## 📍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All GitHub repository spaces (issues, discussions, pull requests)
- Project communication channels (Discord, Slack, email)
- Community events (conferences, meetups, video calls)
- Social media interactions related to the project
- Anywhere community members represent the project

## 🚨 Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

### Option 1: Private Email (Recommended)

Send detailed report to maintainer:

**Email:** [Contact via GitHub profile](https://github.com/tusharkkp)

**Include in your report:**
- What happened (specific incident)
- Who was involved
- When it occurred
- Where it occurred (GitHub issue #, discussion, etc.)
- Any relevant links or screenshots
- How it affected you
- Any documentation or witnesses

### Option 2: GitHub Issues

Create a private security advisory if it involves sensitive information:
- Go to: Settings → Security → Private vulnerability reporting
- Report with context and impact

### Option 3: Moderation Team

If you feel comfortable, mention `@maintainers` in issues/discussions:
- Describe the violation
- Provide specific references
- Explain the impact

---

## 📋 Response Process

### 1. Acknowledgment (24 hours)
- We will confirm receipt of your report
- We will not disclose your identity without permission

### 2. Initial Assessment (48 hours)
- We will evaluate the severity
- We will gather additional information if needed
- We will determine if it's a violation

### 3. Investigation (5-7 days)
- We may reach out to involved parties
- We will document findings
- We will determine appropriate action

### 4. Resolution (7-14 days)
- **For minor violations:** Warning + education
- **For moderate violations:** Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
- **For severe violations:** Permanent ban from community
- We will communicate resolution to all parties

### 5. Follow-up (30 days)
- We will check in on resolution
- We will ensure harassment has stopped
- We will support affected parties

## 🎯 Examples of Good Behavior

### ✅ Constructive Code Review

```
"I noticed this could be optimized. Here's a suggestion:
[code example]
What do you think about this approach?"
```

### ✅ Welcoming Newcomer

```
"Welcome! Great to see you contributing. 
I'm happy to help if you have questions. 
Here are some resources that might help: [links]"
```

### ✅ Handling Disagreement

```
"I see your point, but I have a different perspective.
Let's discuss the trade-offs:
1. Your approach: [benefits/drawbacks]
2. My suggestion: [benefits/drawbacks]
What do you think?"
```

### ✅ Asking for Help

```
"I'm new to this project. I'd like to help with [feature],
but I need guidance. Can someone point me to resources?
Thank you!"
```

---

## 🚫 Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

### ❌ Dismissive Comment

```
"That's a dumb idea. You clearly don't understand this project."
```

### ❌ Gatekeeping

```
"You're not experienced enough to contribute here.
You should go learn more before coming back."
```

### ❌ Harassment

```
"Your questions are annoying. Stop bothering us."
```

### ❌ Discrimination

```
"People like you don't belong in tech."
```

### Project Maintainers

- Maintain confidentiality of reports

- Lead by example

### Community Members
- Report violations when witnessed
- Support affected community members
- Help create inclusive culture
- Challenge unacceptable behavior respectfully
- Welcome newcomers warmly
- Share knowledge generously
